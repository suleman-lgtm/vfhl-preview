Where it goes

Small budget, wide reach.

As an all-volunteer effort, we keep costs lean. Your support pays for the things volunteers cannot donate: film licensing, the Zoom and streaming technology, professional captioning, and the editing that turns each live salon into a lasting, watchable record for the public.
