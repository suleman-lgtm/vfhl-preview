Your gift keeps the conversation going

We are entirely volunteer-run. Every dollar goes to the films, the technology, and the editing that lets thousands witness these discussions, long after the salon ends.

Why monthly matters

A salon happens every single month. Monthly giving is what lets us plan, license films, and say yes to the next conversation without scrambling for funds.

•

Every dollar is visible. As an all-volunteer effort, your gift pays only for films, technology, captioning, and editing, never overhead.

•

It reaches thousands. Each edited salon lives on, freely, for everyone who could not be in the room.

•

You can give in seconds. No account, no friction, cancel or change anytime.

•

Fully tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt by email the moment you give.

Where it goes

Small budget, wide reach.

We keep costs lean so your generosity does the maximum good. Your support pays for the things volunteers cannot donate: film licensing, the streaming technology, professional captioning for accessibility, and the editing that turns each live salon into a lasting, watchable record for the public.
