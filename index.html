Our purpose

A coalition of over a hundred interfaith and interdenominational organizations, Christian, Jewish, Muslim, Unitarian, and Quaker, using film and honest conversation to advance human rights, justice, and peace in the Holy Land.

Non-political, faith-rooted, and based in the Washington, D.C. metropolitan region.
