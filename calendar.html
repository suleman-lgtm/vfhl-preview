How a salon works

Register, watch, and join the conversation.

Register in advance and you will receive the link by email. Each salon opens with a documentary film, followed by a moderated panel and live audience questions. For the final half hour, you are welcome to stay for small breakout conversations with the panelists and fellow attendees.
