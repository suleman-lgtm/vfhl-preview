About us

Film, faith, and the courage to listen

For more than ten years we have brought people together for honest, difficult, necessary conversations about the Holy Land.

Voices From the Holy Land is a coalition of over one hundred interfaith and interdenominational organizations, Christian, Jewish, Muslim, Unitarian, and Quaker, across the Washington, D.C. metropolitan region.

What we do

Each month we screen a carefully chosen documentary film, then open the floor to a panel of nationally and internationally known experts and an audience that brings its whole heart. We are non-political and faith-rooted, and we believe that understanding begins with the willingness to sit and listen to a story that is not your own.

Why it matters

After three generations, peace in the Holy Land remains painfully out of reach. We cannot solve that from a screening room. But we can do the patient, human work that any lasting peace is built on: hearing the differing narratives, holding them with respect, and refusing to look away.

We are demonstrably making a difference, one conversation, one relationship, one changed mind at a time.
