Tell us your answers, just talk

To finish your new website assistant, we need a few answers. You do not have to type. Tap Speak on any question and say your answer out loud, the words appear as text you can fix. When you are done, tap Send.

How this works

  1. Tap the green Speak button under a question.
  2. Allow the microphone if your browser asks (one time).
  3. Talk normally. Tap Stop when finished with that answer.
  4. Skip anything you are unsure of. Your progress saves automatically.
  5. At the bottom, tap Send my answers.

On a phone? You can also tap the little microphone on your keyboard to talk into any box. Best on Google Chrome on a computer or Android.

Your current browser does not support tap-to-talk buttons. No problem: you can still type, or tap the microphone key on your phone's keyboard to dictate into each box. For the smoothest experience, open this page in Google Chrome.
